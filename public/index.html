Open-source E-Ink firmware

A better reading experience for your Xteink

CrossPoint Reader is community-built firmware that replaces the stock software on Xteink X3 and X4 e-readers. More features, more control, fully open-source.

Features

Everything the stock firmware should have been

Built by readers, for readers. CrossPoint adds the features and polish that make your Xteink worth using every day.

EPUB 2 & 3 support
Full EPUB rendering with embedded CSS, images, and proper chapter navigation.
WiFi book uploads
Transfer books wirelessly from any device on your network. Calibre plugin support included.
KOReader Sync
Sync your reading progress across devices with KOReader's open sync protocol.
Customizable typography
Choose from Bookerly, Noto Sans, and Open Dyslexic. Adjust size, line spacing, margins, and alignment.
Multiple themes
Switch between Classic, Lyra, and Lyra Extended UI themes. Portrait and landscape rotation supported.
Multi-language text
Latin and Cyrillic script support covering English, Spanish, French, German, Russian, Ukrainian, and more.
Reading persistence
Automatically saves your position. Pick up right where you left off, every time.
OTA updates
Update firmware over WiFi or flash directly from this page. No special tools required.

Web flasher

Flash from your browser

Connect your Xteink via USB and flash firmware directly. No downloads, no installs, just Chrome or Edge on desktop.

Browser not supported. WebSerial requires Chrome or Edge on desktop. Please switch browsers to use the web flasher.

Device Model

Proceed with caution. Take a backup using Save full flash before flashing. Avoid disconnecting your device or closing the tab during a flash operation.

OTA Fast Flash

Writes firmware to the backup OTA partition and swaps boot. Your settings and books are preserved.
