Convert TrueType or OpenType fonts to CrossPoint's .cpfont format directly in your browser. Includes kerning and ligature support. No tools to install.

Beta feature: Custom SD card fonts require the CrossPoint beta firmware installed on your device.

Letters, numbers, spaces, and dashes. Shown on the reader as one font family.

Generated Files

    How to install

    1. Download the generated .cpfont files (or the zip).
    2. Connect to your reader's WiFi and open the web interface.
    3. Go to the Fonts page and upload each file, or copy them to the SD card at /.crosspoint/fonts/YourFont/. (Make sure you toggle show hidden files on your OS.)
    4. The font will appear in your reader's font settings.
    Note: Glyph rasterisation uses your browser's Canvas renderer. Results may differ slightly from the official font packs which use FreeType.
